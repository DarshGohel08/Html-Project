    North Indian Cuisine

    North india is known for its rich,aromatic,and flavorful dishes often cooked with ghee and spices:

    • Biryani : A fragment rise dish made with aromatic spices and meat or vagetables.
    • Butter Chicken : A creamy,tomato-based curry tht's favorite wordwide.
    • Paneer tikka : Marinated Paneer cubes grilled to perfaction.

    south Indian Cuisine

    South India offer a wide array of dishes with a perfect blend of spices and tangy flavors.

    • Masala Dosa : A crispy rise crepe stuffed with spice potato filling.
    • Idli and Samber : Soft rice cakes served with a tangy lentil-based vegitable stew.
    • Chettinad Chicken : A fiery,flavorful chicken dish from Tamil Nadu.

    sweet Dishes

    No Indian meal is completed without including in some mouth-watering desserts:

    • Gulab jamun : Fried milk solids soaked in sugar syrup.
    • Rasgula : Soft and spongy cheese balls in light sugar syrup.
    • Payasam : A South Indian desserst made of milk ,rise,and jaggery.
